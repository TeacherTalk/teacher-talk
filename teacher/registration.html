Welcome to TeacherTalk!

TeacherTalk is an anonymous website that allows students to talk to their teachers without revealing the student's identity. TeacherTalk is intended for students to be able to talk to teachers they feel comfortable with about personal problems that they are having. It is not meant to be a place for homework help, rude or intrusive questions, or other misuses. If you feel a student's problems are beyond what you can personally help with, you are encouraged to flag the conversation which will send the chat history, which includes all messages, to the school counselor so that they can use their professional skills to help the student. When you flag a conversation, this will reveal the student's identity for their own safety. Please do not misuse this function as it is only intended to be used when a student is in a crisis or poses a danger to themself or others. You are able to opt in by checking the box labeled "yes" below. By checking the "yes" box, you are affirming that you have read the previous paragraph and are willing to receive messages from students. You can opt out of participating by checking the "no" box below and you will not receive messages from students.
